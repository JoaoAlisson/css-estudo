Diferença Flex x Grid

>>>>>>>>>> FLEX <<<<<<<<<<<
        - flex-direction: column;
        - justify-content: flex-end;
        - align-items: flex-end;
        - flex-wrap: wrap;
        - align-content: flex-start;
        - flex-flow: row wrap;
        - flex-basis: 400px;
        - flex: 1 1 400px;
        - order: 1;
        - flex-grow: 1;
        - flex-shrink: 1;
        - align-self: center;

>>>>>>>>>> GRID <<<<<<<<<<<
        - O que é a grid
        - grid-template-columns (px, %, rem, em fr) repeat(3, 300%) repeat(auto-fil, 1fr) repeat(auto-fil, minmax(300px, 1fr))
        - grid-template-rows
        - grid-template-areas
        - columns-gap, rows-gap, gap
        - grid-column-start
        - grid-column-end
        - grid-column: 2/4
        - grid-row-start
        - grid-row-end
        - grid-row: 2/4
        - Alinhamentos:
                JUSTIFY (Hor) e ALIGN (Vert)
                Container:
                        justify-content
                        align-content
                Itens: 
                        justify-items
                        align-items
                Item:
                        justify-self
                        align-self
